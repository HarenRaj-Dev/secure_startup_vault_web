About Secure Startup Vault

Secure Startup Vault was developed as a final year collegiate project to bridge the gap between accessible cloud storage and high-level enterprise security. In an era where data breaches are becoming commonplace, our mission was to create a platform where startups can store sensitive intellectual property without fear of unauthorized access.

Technical Excellence

Our architecture is built on the robust Flask framework, utilizing a PostgreSQL backend hosted on Neon.tech for serverless scalability.

We implemented a hybrid encryption model:

  • RSA-2048: Used for asymmetric identity verification and key exchange.

  • Fernet (AES-128): Used for high-speed symmetric encryption of data blobs.

  • Vercel Serverless: Ensuring 99.9% uptime and global availability.
