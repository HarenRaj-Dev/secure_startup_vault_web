Privacy Policy

Zero-Knowledge Protocol: Our developers cannot see your data. Your files are encrypted with keys that only you control.

1. Commitment to Data Privacy

At Secure Startup Vault, we believe that privacy is a fundamental right. This policy outlines how we handle your information in our serverless cloud environment. Because this project was built with a "Privacy by Design" philosophy, we have engineered the system to ensure that the development team has no way to view the contents of your files. Our infrastructure is optimized to provide maximum security while minimizing the collection of personal metadata. We treat your digital footprint with the same level of care that we would expect for our own sensitive information.

2. Information Collection and Data Minimization

We collect only the information necessary to provide a secure login experience. This includes your email address, which serves as your unique identifier, and a hashed version of your password. We do not collect names, phone numbers, or physical addresses. Our application does not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes; the only cookies used are strictly "session cookies" required to keep you logged into the Flask environment and to prevent Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF). By minimizing the data we collect, we significantly reduce the risk to our users in the event of any external service disruption.

3. Advanced Encryption Standards

Your privacy is protected by a multi-layered cryptographic stack. When you upload a file, the following process occurs: first, the file is encrypted locally using the Fernet symmetric algorithm. Second, the resulting data "blob" is secured and sent via an SSL-encrypted tunnel (HTTPS) provided by Vercel. Finally, the data is stored in our PostgreSQL database at Neon.tech. Because the decryption keys are tied to your user session and hashed credentials, your files are protected even in the event of a total database breach. Our use of salt and pepper in hashing ensures that even identical passwords result in different stored signatures.

4. Use of Cloud Infrastructure

To ensure the scalability of this college project, we utilize Vercel for web hosting and Neon.tech for database management. These providers are leaders in cloud security and comply with international standards. While your data resides on their servers, it remains in an encrypted state (at-rest) and is never accessible to the employees of these companies. We do not share, sell, or rent your email address to any third-party marketing agencies. Our partnership with these cloud leaders ensures that the Secure Startup Vault benefits from world-class physical and digital security perimeters around the clock.

5. User Rights and Data Portability

Under the principles of modern data protection, you have the right to access the data stored in your vault at any time. You also have the "Right to be Forgotten." If you choose to delete your account, our system is programmed to remove all database entries associated with your ID immediately. This includes your email, your encrypted blobs, and your cryptographic keys. As the Team Leader, Haren Raj ensures that no "ghost" copies of your data are kept on any backup drives or secondary servers. We believe in complete transparency regarding the lifecycle of your data from the moment of upload to the moment of deletion.
